The service

Hills Roof Care is a residential roof maintenance subscription offered by SBMN Operating Pty Ltd (trading as JSJ Roofing). The service includes scheduled gutter cleaning and annual roof inspection for single-dwelling residential properties in serviced suburbs of Sydney's Hills District.

What's included

  • Two professional gutter cleans per 12-month subscription period
  • One full roof inspection with photo report per 12-month period
  • Priority booking for additional services
  • 10% discount on repair work identified during inspection and completed by JSJ Roofing

What's not included

Repairs, replacement of damaged roofing materials, pressure washing, second-storey homes requiring specialist access equipment (quoted separately), and properties outside the serviced area.

Subscription terms

Monthly plans have a 12-month minimum commitment. After 12 months, you may cancel at any time with 30 days' notice. Annual plans are paid upfront and do not auto-renew without your explicit consent. No refunds are issued after the first service has been delivered.

Scheduling

Services are available on Wednesdays only. You must book at least 5 business days in advance. JSJ Roofing reserves the right to reschedule due to unsafe weather conditions, with at least 24 hours' notice where possible.

Access requirements

You must ensure safe and unobstructed access to gutters and roof areas on the booked date. If access is not available and we cannot complete the service, the visit will be counted as used.

Limitation of liability

JSJ Roofing's liability is limited to the value of services paid in the current subscription period. We are not liable for pre-existing damage, damage caused by weather events, or consequential losses.
