Workmanship Warranty

JSJ Roofing warrants that all roofing services provided under a Hills Roof Care subscription or one-off booking will be free from defects in workmanship for the following periods:

Service TypeWarranty Duration
Repointing5 years from completion
General Workmanship2 years from completion
Non-Structural Repairs2 years from completion

What the Warranty Covers

The warranty covers defects in workmanship directly attributable to JSJ Roofing's service. If a covered defect is identified within the warranty period, JSJ Roofing will inspect and, where the claim is valid, repair or replace the defective work at no charge.

Warranty Exclusions

This warranty does not cover:

  • Acts of God — storms, hail, earthquakes, hurricanes, or fires
  • Natural wear and tear, fading, or cosmetic deterioration
  • Damage caused by unauthorised foot traffic on the roof after service completion
  • Damage caused by solar panel installers, painters, or other contractors not authorised by JSJ Roofing
  • Misuse, negligence, or failure to maintain the property
  • Structural movement, ceiling cracks, or internal water damage unless directly resulting from JSJ Roofing's workmanship

Voiding Conditions

The warranty will be void if:

  • Unauthorised individuals walk on the roof after service completion
  • Any alterations or repairs are carried out by parties other than JSJ Roofing
  • Damage is caused by third-party contractors (e.g. solar installers, painters) not authorised by JSJ Roofing

How to Make a Warranty Claim

To lodge a warranty claim:

  • Email info@jsjroofing.com.au with the subject line "Warranty Claim"
  • Include your original invoice as proof of work
  • Describe the issue and include photos where possible

JSJ Roofing will inspect the issue within a reasonable timeframe and advise whether the claim is valid under these terms.

Limitation of Liability

JSJ Roofing's liability under this warranty is limited to repairing or replacing the defective part of the service. We are not liable for incidental or consequential damages, including property damage or business interruption.

Non-Transferability

This warranty applies exclusively to the original purchaser listed on the invoice and is non-transferable.
