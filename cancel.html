Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · SBMN Operating Pty Ltd (JSJ Roofing) · ABN 26 667 426 593

Monthly plan

The monthly plan has a 12-month minimum commitment. You may cancel at any time after the 12-month minimum by emailing info@jsjroofing.com.au with 30 days' notice. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing month. No partial-month refunds are issued.

Annual plan

The annual plan is paid upfront. It does not auto-renew — we will contact you before the end of your plan year to ask if you'd like to continue. Refunds are not available after the first service has been delivered. If no services have been delivered, a full refund is available within 14 days of purchase.

Single clean

Single clean bookings may be cancelled or rescheduled up to 48 hours before the booked time at no charge. Cancellations within 48 hours forfeit the booking fee.

How to cancel

Email info@jsjroofing.com.au with your name, address, and "Cancel my Hills Roof Care plan" in the subject line. We'll confirm cancellation within 2 business days.
