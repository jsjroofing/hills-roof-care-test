Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · SBMN Operating Pty Ltd (JSJ Roofing) · ABN 26 667 426 593

What information we collect

When you subscribe to Hills Roof Care or book a service, we collect your name, email address, phone number, service address, and payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we do not store card numbers). We also collect booking details via Cal.com.

How we use your information

We use your information to deliver the service you've subscribed to, send booking confirmations and reminders, provide photo inspection reports, and contact you about your account. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Third-party services

We use Stripe for payment processing (subject to Stripe's privacy policy), Cal.com for booking management, and GitHub Pages for website hosting. Each of these services has its own privacy policy.

Data retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active and for up to 7 years after cancellation for tax and legal compliance purposes.

Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Contact us at info@jsjroofing.com.au to exercise these rights.
