Questions answered before you ask.

What's actually included in each gutter clean?
We clear all gutters of leaves, debris and silt by hand, flush downpipes to confirm they're flowing, bag and remove the waste, and send you photos before and after. We don't pressure-wash gutters (it pushes debris into downpipes and can damage older roofing materials).
What's the roof inspection?
While we're up there for one of your cleans, we do a full visual inspection: tiles, ridge capping, flashings, valleys, sealant condition, signs of leaks or storm damage. You get a photo report within 24 hours. If we find something, you decide what to do about it — no pressure, and 10% off if you choose us for the repair.
Why a subscription instead of just calling when I need it?
Honestly? Because it stops being your problem. The data on home maintenance is depressing: most people forget about gutters until water's coming down a wall. Two cleans a year on autopilot prevents 90% of avoidable roof damage, and an annual inspection catches the rest before it costs five figures.
Can I cancel?
Yes. The monthly plan has a 12-month minimum, then you can cancel anytime through your account. The annual plan is non-refundable after the first service, but it doesn't auto-renew without your say-so. We're not interested in trapping people who don't want to be here.
What if the weather's bad on my booked day?
We'll get in touch the day before to reschedule. Roof work in the rain is dangerous and a waste of everyone's morning. We'll find another Wednesday that suits.
What if I don't need a clean exactly six months in?
Push it out. We'll send a reminder, but you choose when. The plan covers two cleans per twelve months — flexible on timing.
What does the $108/month cover, exactly?
Two professional gutter cleans, one annual roof inspection with photo report, priority booking, and 10% off any repair work we identify and you choose to have done by us. It does not cover repairs themselves, replacement of damaged parts, or gutter cleans on a second property.
Is this just for Castle Hill?
We're starting here. If demand's there, we'll expand to Cherrybrook, Baulkham Hills, Glenhaven and the rest of the Hills District over the next year. Sign up now even if you're outside Castle Hill and we'll be in touch when we're in your suburb.
